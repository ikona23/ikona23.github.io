Reflection

1.7.2016

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

I am learning what I need from day one. It's good to have structure and a work-like environment and that's what I was missing in my previous confused solitary dev life. I am here to give it my all - learn as much I can and share it with my fellow students.

What are your impressions?

I am very impressed and humbled by the people, that I've had the chance to meet in EDA. It's well thought out and organized learning structure.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I think I can "survive", grow and better myself with killer web-dev skills. I like the forum environment, and I'm happy to be working with like-minded people. I can't wait what next weeks will brings.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

Not really. Google prepared me enough :) Of course I had the chance to meet some people from EDA and I think they are cool and lovely.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Yes, it does make me nervous, and I'm also excited. I already went through my first thrill. I also have a little bit anxiaty speaking and writing english. I promise, I will get better.
