Sprint 1 - Time and habits

2.7.2016

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I am trying the pomodoro technique with a combination of growing small habits. After every hour of learning, I spent 15 minutes of working around the house, or just a bit of drawing. If everything is going well, I keep going. I have been also meditating for some years and find it a very comforting place to be. Mindfulness and realizing that life is breathing through me, gives me special moments in my life.

What did you find interesting?

A technique like pomodoro gives me more focus and my mind feels sharp. I do not feel that I have to stop. Since I am doing a little bit of exercise after every blog (push-ups), I found that my body is a bit sore :).

What worked for you?

Meditation and mindfulness always worked for me.

Which will you experiment further with?

I want to keep working on little habits and keep meditating.
